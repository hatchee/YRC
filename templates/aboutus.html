Company Profile

Tibetdiscovery.com (online since 2010) provides online Tibet travel services directly to the end consumers for English speaking people from all over the world.

As the leading local tour operator in Tibet, we have a long-established relationship with the hotels, restaurants, and transportation companies of Tibet, and can provide guaranteed service at a lower price.

Since we are a local company selling tours directly to the end consumers through the Internet, the prices of our Tibet tours are regularly 10%-20% lower than our competitors from Chinese cities like Shanghai, Guilin, Beijing, etc., and 15%-25% lower than overseas travel agents or wholesalers.
